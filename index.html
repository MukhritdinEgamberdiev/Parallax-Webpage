National clothes

The style of Uzbek traditional dress remains unchanged up to the present day. The men wear shirts and trousers topped off by dressing gowns (khalat.). The gowns are very light and made from cotton. They have vents cut on each side of the robe to expedite walking. The trousers are cut wide. Female clothes include dressing gowns and brightly patterned dresses.
